dressings + sauces

Mustard Vinaigrette
Measurement Description
1/2 c. white balsamic vinegar
1 T. honey
1 T. Dijon mustard
1/2 tsp. pepper
2 tsp. salt
2 tsp. garlic, minced
1/2 c. olive oil

Mix ingredients until thoroughly combined. Chill until serving.

Enchilada Sauce
Measurement Description
1 T. tomato paste
1/4 c. flour
3 T. chili powder
1 1/2 tsp. salt
1/4 tsp. pepper
4 c. water

Brown tomato paste in sauce pan on MH. Mix dry ingredients separately and slowly whisk in cold water. Add water + dry ingredient mixture to sauce pan and whisk into paste. Stir constantly on MH/M until sauce boils + thickens.
