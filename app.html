buffalo chicken dip
Measurement Description
2 10 oz. cans chunk chicken, drained
2 8 oz. pkg cream cheese, softened
1 c. ranch dressing
3/4 c. Frank's Red Hot
1 1/2 c. cheddar cheese, shredded

Heat chicken and hot sauce over medium heat until heated through. Stir in cream cheese + ranch until well blended. Mix in half of shredded cheese and transfer mixture to slow cooker. Sprinkle remaining cheese over the top. Cover and cook on Low until hot + bubbly.
