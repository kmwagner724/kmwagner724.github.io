Soups

Chili
Measurement Description
1/2 lb. ground beef or turkey
1 green pepper, chopped
1 onion, chopped
3 cloves garlic, chopped
2 large cans chopped tomatoes
2-15 oz. canned beans (kidney, back, navy)
1/2 can tomato paste
1 c. water
2 T. chili powder
1/4 tsp. cayenne pepper
1 tsp. dried basil
hot sauce
salt + pepper

Brown ground meat with pepper and onion. Add tomatoes, drained beans + remaining ingredients. Simmer 30 min. Season with salt + pepper to taste.

Healthy Chicken Salad
Measurement Description
2 c. grilled chicken, cubed
2 c. grapes, halved
1 c. Swiss cheese, juilliened
1 c. slivered almonds
2/3 c. Greek yogurt
1/3 c. light mayo
salt + pepper

Combine all ingredients. Season with salt + pepper to taste.
